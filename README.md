An AI-powered mental health support web application designed to provide empathetic, accessible, and stigma-free emotional support for teenagers and young adults.

Overview

Mindful Companion is a conversational AI therapist web application that offers users a safe space to express their thoughts, reflect on emotions, and receive supportive responses. The project was created in response to the increasing mental health challenges faced by teenagers, including academic stress, loneliness, anxiety, and burnout.

While not a replacement for licensed mental health professionals, Mindful Companion acts as a first-line emotional support system and encourages healthy coping mechanisms and self-awareness.

 Objectives

Provide immediate emotional support in a non-judgmental environment

Encourage self-reflection and emotional expression

Reduce stigma around discussing mental health

Offer accessible mental health assistance for teens

Demonstrate ethical and responsible AI development
